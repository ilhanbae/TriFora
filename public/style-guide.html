Size, Position & Interaction Guidelines for Popups & Modals:

Popups and Modals

All of the following UI elements should have a border and curved edges:

Popup Boxes

This will appear when a user is attempting to complete an irreversible action. The popup will contain the options yes, no, or cancel, one of which must be chosen.

Ex. Termination of an account, post, or Removable of a friend

×

Are you sure?

Drop Down Menu

Dropdowns are useful when a single action could have multiple outcomes

Ex: Upon clicking your profile picture, you can choose to go to your profile, check notifications, or log out

Toast

This will appear as a temporary 5 second notification serving as feedback on certain actions

Ex. Sending a friend request, Post reported, or Logged in

Feedback in cases of Success and Failure

Success

Upon successfully performing a task, a temporary notification with a blueish color will appear on the bottom left side of the user's screen for 5 seconds

Ex: Upon the completion of editing your profile, you may receive the message "Profile Updated Successfully!"

Profile Updated Successfully!

×

Failure

Upon unsuccessfully performing a task, a temporary notification with a redish color will appear on the bottom left side of the user's screen for 5 seconds

Ex: Trying to submit edits to your profile that don't work may prompt the message "Something went wrong"

Something went wrong!

×

Methods for Inline Help

Warning

If an error is run into, a warning message will occur that will stay on the screen for about 10 seconds.

Ex: Trying to create a post in a community a user is not part of might prompt the message "Join Community"
